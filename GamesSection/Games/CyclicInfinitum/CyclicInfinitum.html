Cyclic Infinitum is a 2D space shooter where your ship is made of blocks. While progressing in the game you can change and improve your ship by finding new blocks and changing your ship

You and your crew will have to take on an epic journey traveling through multiple worlds on a quest to defeat the emperor. You will also have to manage your crew and your resources to succeed.

The game also features:

Randomly Generated Music
Procedural Maps
Ship parts editor

Cyclic inifinitum has been created
with libgdx and java. And it's
completly free to download and play
